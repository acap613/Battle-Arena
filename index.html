Warrior

Warriors have higher health and stamina, however their strength also makes them slower and clumsier.

Rogue

Rogues are fast and have high stamina and speed, which makes up for their lack of strength and health.

Mage

Mages are glass cannons who put all their effort into spells. this means they suck at everything else.

Hunter

Hunters are well rounded fighters that focus on evening out their skills. NO strengths or weaknesses.
